Bridging the gender imbalance in Tech

by digify techSept 8th, 2021

Growing up, I was constantly exposed to the gender inequality that plagued the tech industry, and I was determined to do something about it. me and my team began to research different ways we could help address this issue, and eventually Digify was born. creating a generation of digital intelligence


The goal of Digify is to provide entrepreneurship opportunities specifically to women, in order to bridge the gender imbalance within the tech sector

The Vision


Entrepreneurship opportunities to women who seek to venture in the technology space


Research showed that While the gender gap in labor force participation worldwide has declined sharply over the last thirty years(decreased by 2.1% from 2020 to 2021), women in low- and middle-income countries (LMICs) like Malawi continue to be underrepresented in the formal sector.

Mission


 to provide training in digital skills that help generate income. We started with web development and graphic designing, and we have since expanded to providing more training in other digital skills like python and android programming.
